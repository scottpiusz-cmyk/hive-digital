# Purpose

Hive exists to simplify complex international document requirements for people who need to work, teach, immigrate or relocate internationally.

Rather than simply processing documents, Hive consults with clients to determine the correct documentation strategy before work begins, helping clients avoid unnecessary costs, delays and rejected applications.

---

# Mission

To become the most trusted cross-border documentation partner in the Asia-Pacific region.

---

# Long-Term Vision

Hive will become the leading specialist for international background checks, apostilles and document authentication across selected APAC markets.

Rather than expanding globally, Hive will focus on becoming the most trusted provider within a limited number of countries where expertise, reputation and partnerships create sustainable competitive advantage.

Initial expansion markets:

- China
- South Korea
- Japan

Potential future markets:

- Vietnam
- Thailand

---

# Core Customers

Hive primarily serves:

- International Schools
- Universities
- Recruiters
- HR Departments
- International Educators
- Expatriates

These customers operate within an ecosystem where people regularly relocate between countries and therefore require ongoing document services throughout their careers.

# Competitive Advantage

Hive does not simply process requested documents.

Hive first seeks to understand:

- the client's goal
- destination country
- employer requirements
- local jurisdiction requirements
- timeline

Only then is a recommendation made.

Success is measured by successful document acceptance—not by simply completing an order.

---

# Customer Promise

We believe:

- Recommend the correct solution, not the most expensive one.
- If there is a cheaper legitimate option, tell the client.
- If Hive makes a mistake, Hive fixes it.
- Communicate clearly throughout the process.
- Give honest estimates of time and cost.
- Build long-term relationships through trust.

# Geographic Expansion Strategy

Hive will remain operationally lean.

Expansion will occur through carefully selected regional service partners rather than building large office networks.

Hive owns the customer relationship.

Regional partners assist with service delivery while maintaining Hive's quality standards.

---

# Technology Strategy

Technology should improve customer experience rather than replace expertise.

Future priorities include:

- AI consultation assistant
- WhatsApp integration
- WeChat integration
- LINE integration
- KakaoTalk integration

AI should answer common questions, qualify enquiries and gather information before handing complex cases to a human specialist.
