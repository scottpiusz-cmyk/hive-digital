Vietnam Focus

Vietnam Document
Legalization & Authentication

Vietnam acceded to the Hague Apostille Convention on December 31, 2025. The Convention enters into force for Vietnam on September 30, 2026. For documents needed before this date: Vietnamese Embassy authentication (legalization) is required. For documents needed after this date: Hague Apostille is accepted. Hive Digital handles both pathways for American professionals and employers.

Two Pathways for Vietnam

Before Sept 30, 2026

Embassy Authentication (Legalization)

Documents must be legalized at the Vietnamese Embassy or Consulate General in the country of origin. This is a multi-step authentication process.

After Sept 30, 2026

Hague Apostille

Apostille from the issuing state's competent authority is sufficient. No Vietnamese Embassy visit required.
