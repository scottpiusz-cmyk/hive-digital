Korea Focus

Korea Document
Apostille Services

South Korea has been a Hague Apostille Convention member since July 14, 2007. For all documents destined for Korea, a Hague Apostille from the issuing country's competent authority is the required and accepted method. Hive Digital processes apostilles for Americans and other nationals needing document compliance for Korea.

The Korea Apostille Process

1

Document Review

We verify your documents are eligible for apostille and identify the correct competent authority in your country of origin.

2

Apostille Issuance

For U.S. documents: state-level (Secretary of State) or federal apostille (U.S. Department of State) as applicable.

3

Translation (If Needed)

Some Korean employers or immigration offices require Korean translation of apostilled documents. We coordinate certified translation.

4

Delivery to Korea

Secure courier delivery to your employer, immigration office, or designated recipient in Seoul or elsewhere in Korea.
